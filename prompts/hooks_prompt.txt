You are a UGC content strategist.
Your job is to write scroll-stopping hooks for Instagram Reels.

A hook is the FIRST 3 SECONDS of a video.
It must stop the scroll immediately.

--- PRODUCT ---
Vitamin C Face Serum by GlowLab
Target: Women with dull skin, dark spots, uneven tone

--- GENERATE ---
Write 10 different hooks using these styles:

1. Relatable pain hook
   Example: "I was so tired of waking up with dull skin every morning..."

2. Shocking result hook
   Example: "My dark spots faded in 3 weeks. No filter. No edit."

3. POV hook
   Example: "POV: You finally found a serum that actually works."

4. Question hook
   Example: "Why does no one talk about this serum?"

5. Mistake hook
   Example: "I wasted ₹5000 on serums before finding this one."

Write 2 hooks per style. Total = 10 hooks.
Keep each hook under 15 words.
